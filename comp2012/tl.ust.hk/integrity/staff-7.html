Excluding Cheaters from Course Evaluation

To prevent students who are found cheating from retaliating against the instructor, any student penalized for cheating in a course can be excluded from participating in the end-of-term evaluation of the course concerned.

The department should send all such requests to CELT by using a standard form on or before week 11 in a regular term. Courses that end early should make a corresponding allowance for the submission of such requests.
